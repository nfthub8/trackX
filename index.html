FREQUENTLY ASKED

TrackX NFTs are minted on the Ethereum blockchain using the ERC-721A standard for gas-optimized batch minting. We plan to bridge to Polygon in Phase 4 for reduced transaction costs.
Visit our whitelist registration page, fill in your details and wallet address. Priority access is given to early Discord members and social media engagers. 2,000 whitelist spots are available.
Holders receive: DAO governance voting rights, exclusive access to IRL F1 race events, $APEX token staking rewards, early access to Season 2 mints, and access to a holder-only racing metaverse experience.
TrackX features 7 rarity tiers: Common (50%), Uncommon (25%), Rare (12%), Epic (7%), Legendary (4%), Mythic (1.5%), and Ultra Rare (0.5%). Each tier unlocks additional real-world and on-chain benefits.
TrackX charges a 5% royalty on all secondary market sales. Of this, 2% goes directly to the holder community treasury (DAO), 2% to ongoing development, and 1% to partnered F1 charities.
